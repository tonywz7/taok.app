# Taok Brand Guide

## Brand Identity

**Company Name**: Taok
**Product**: AI-native GTM Workspace
**Positioning**: Research before every outreach decision
**Tagline**: Intelligence → Execution

---

## Core Values (In Order)

1. **Intelligence** - Evidence-based, research-driven decisions
2. **Execution** - Fast, confident GTM action
3. **Research** - Deep, verified market and company knowledge
4. **Trust** - Enterprise-grade security and compliance
5. **Enterprise** - Built for serious business
6. **Speed** - Real-time research and signals
7. **Clarity** - No marketing hype, no AI magic talk

## Product Terminology

### Primary Concepts (Use These Terms)

| Term | Definition | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Workspace | The main product container where research happens | "Launch your Taok workspace" |
| Research | Core user activity; investigating companies and people | "Run AI research on your target accounts" |
| Project | A collection of research on a specific market or segment | "Create a project for European fintech companies" |
| Company | A prospect or account being researched | "Research 50 companies in your TAM" |
| People | Decision makers and contacts at companies | "Discover 200+ people across your target accounts" |
| Signals | Indicators of buying intent, expansion, or leadership changes | "Get real-time buying signals" |
| Agents | AI assistants that autonomously execute research workflows | "Deploy an agent to monitor hiring signals" |
| Knowledge | Compiled research insights and company intelligence | "Build shared knowledge about your market" |
| Insights | Actionable intelligence extracted from research | "Extract insights about market trends" |
| Sources | External data providers and platforms we integrate with | "We pull from 50+ data sources" |
| Evidence | Verified, cited data points supporting research conclusions | "Every finding includes evidence and sources" |
| Findings | Output of a research session; curated intelligence | "Export your findings to Salesforce" |
| Automation | Workflows that execute research tasks repeatedly | "Create automation for your research pipeline" |
| Playbooks | Pre-built research templates for common GTM scenarios | "Use our enterprise playbook template" |

### Secondary Concepts (Use When Appropriate)

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| Organization | A company account in Taok with multiple teams |
| Team | A group of GTM professionals collaborating in Workspace |
| Segment | A filtered group of companies matching specific criteria |
| Workflow | A sequence of research steps executed manually or automatically |
| Integration | Connection to external platforms (Salesforce, HubSpot, etc.) |
| Pipeline | Series of research stages leading to outreach |
| Intelligence Layer | The AI and research capabilities powering the product |
| Market | The group of companies Taok helps you understand |
| TAM | Total Addressable Market being researched |
| ICP | Ideal Customer Profile being targeted |

### Terms to NEVER Use

❌ Don't use these:
- "Platform" (use "Workspace")
- "Building" (use "Research")
- "Creating" (use "Researching" or "Discovering")
- "Features" (use "Capabilities")
- "Users" (use "Teams" or "GTM professionals")
- "Customers" (use "GTM teams" or "Sales teams")
- "Integrations" (use "Data sources" or "Connections")
- "Deploying" (use "Running" or "Launching")
- "Scaling" (use "Expanding research" or "Deepening research")
- "Shipping" (use "Executing" or "Outreaching")
- "Connection" (use "Integration" or "Data source")
- "Sync" (use "Integration" or "Pull")

## Voice & Tone

### How to Write Like Taok

**Characteristics**:
- Professional, not playful
- Technical, not marketing-heavy
- Minimal, not wordy
- Confident, not hypey
- Evidence-based, not emotional
- Direct, not flowery

### Good Examples
✅ "Research companies with verified contact information"
✅ "AI agents that monitor buying signals"
✅ "Enterprise-grade research at scale"
✅ "Security built into every layer"
✅ "No credit card required"

### Bad Examples
❌ "Magical AI that changes everything"
❌ "Revolutionary platform for modern teams"
❌ "The future of business is here"
❌ "Transform your workflow instantly"
❌ "Industry-leading innovation"

### Writing Rules

1. **Be specific** - "Research 42M companies" not "Access global data"
2. **Use evidence** - "99.99% uptime" not "Always reliable"
3. **Focus on outcomes** - "Discover leads faster" not "Advanced algorithms"
4. **Avoid hype** - "AI research" not "Magical AI"
5. **Be clear** - "People intelligence" not "Revolutionary insights"
6. **Use data** - "800ms latency" not "Extremely fast"

## Compliance & Messaging

### When Discussing Security
✅ "Bank-grade encryption"
✅ "SOC 2 Type II certified"
✅ "Enterprise-grade security"
✅ "Your data stays yours"
✅ "Prospect data is confidential"

❌ "Military-grade encryption" (overstatement)
❌ "Unhackable" (false claim)
❌ "Zero breaches ever" (unverifiable)

### When Discussing Performance
✅ "800ms average research latency"
✅ "50+ data sources"
✅ "42M companies researched"
✅ "99.99% uptime SLA"

❌ "Faster than competitors" (unverified)
❌ "Instantaneous results" (false)
❌ "The fastest platform" (subjective)

### When Discussing AI
✅ "AI-powered research"
✅ "AI agents"
✅ "AI-native workspace"
✅ "Machine learning models"

❌ "AI magic" (hype)
❌ "Magical AI" (hype)
❌ "Powered by AGI" (not true)
❌ "Artificial Intelligence that thinks like humans" (not accurate)

## Questions? 

Always ask yourself:
1. **Is this research-focused?** (Not "building" or "creating")
2. **Is this for GTM professionals?** (Not generic "users")
3. **Is this specific and evidence-based?** (Not hype or vague)
4. **Does it follow the voice and tone?** (Professional, minimal, confident)
5. **Is the terminology consistent?** (Using approved Taok terms)

If the answer to any is "no", revise before publishing.
